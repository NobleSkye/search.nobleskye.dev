About Skye Search

Privacy-focused metasearch engine

What is Skye Search?

Skye Search is a privacy-focused metasearch engine that aggregates results from multiple search engines to provide you with comprehensive and unbiased search results. Built with user privacy as our top priority, we don't track, store, or profile our users.

How It Works

Our search engine operates by:

  • Aggregating Results: We query multiple search engines and combine their results to give you a more complete picture
  • Removing Tracking: All queries are anonymized and we strip tracking parameters from results
  • No Data Collection: We don't store your search history, IP addresses, or personal information
  • Open Source Powered: Built on SearXNG, an open-source metasearch engine framework

Privacy Commitment

We believe privacy is a fundamental right. That's why:

  • We never log your search queries
  • We don't use tracking cookies or analytics
  • Your IP address is not stored
  • We don't build user profiles
  • All data is processed anonymously
Third-Party AI Services: When using our AI chat feature, you'll be connecting directly to third-party services (like ChatGPT, Claude, etc.). These services have their own privacy policies and may collect data. We always warn you before connecting to these services.
